Welcome to the FeNNOmix Lab! We are an interdisciplinary research group interested in applying computing technologies as well as artificial intelligence/deep learning to study multi-dimentional proteomics including glycoproteomics, immunopeptidomics, virus-host proteomics/metaproteomics, and PTMomics. See our github: https://github.com/fennomix.
